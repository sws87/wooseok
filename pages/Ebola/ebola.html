Ebolalog

Recontextualized data sources regarding ebola. Despite of the fact that Ebola is the most virulent virus-infecting diseases, it tends to be regarded as a temporary phonomenon happened overseas. The approach was aimed to bring the awareness and seriousness of the situation, and to solve out the disjunction of dots and empathy; graphical rendering of reality. General experience inspired by log, allowing one sentence to represent the timeline as well as the content. The position of dots indicates actual location of each country and the gradual increase of dots are generated by vertical scroll.(GIF images are simple mockup of how it works)
