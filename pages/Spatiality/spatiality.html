WOOSEOK SHUR

Spatiality

For the hypothetical lecture series poster, I chose four sculptors ; Sarah Sze, Jen Stark, Doho Suh, Donald Judd. Space consist of the countless sum of faces, and a face is the sum of lines. A line comes from the connection of dots. To show how the space is formulated, what the relationship among dots, lines, faces and space is, I created 3d letter forms using wires. A small hole, wire, and the letter represent each of the dot, line and space
